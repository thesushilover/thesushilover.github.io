Donuts are some of the most delicious comfort foods in the world. Donuts have a disputed history. There are many different theories suggesting where donuts originated. One theory suggests that they were invented in North America by Dutch travelers. Also, donuts comes from the word oliekoek which means "a sweetened cake fried in fat" Donuts usually have 195 calories and have very little health benefits. For example, it has 0% of Vitamin A, D, B-12, and B-6. Donuts have about 20% to 25% of oil in them too. So, have fun eating them and hopefully you don't get a heart attack!
